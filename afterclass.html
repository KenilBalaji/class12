Choice of the Month — The Quiet Cosmos
By F. Author — 2024
A moving, intimate exploration of people trying to find meaning after a global technological shift. The Quiet Cosmos balances tender character work with quiet, surprising humor — a strong pick for readers who like thoughtful, human science fiction.
